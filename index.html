support businesses —
we build futures.

Neondesk Africa was born from a simple belief: African entrepreneurs deserve world-class support. We exist to bridge the gap between ambition and achievement — giving founders the frameworks, networks, and hands-on guidance that actually move the needle.

Our vision is to become Africa's leading catalyst for entrepreneurship, innovation, and sustainable business growth. Our mission is to reduce the high failure rate of startups and SMEs by equipping founders with practical tools and powerful support networks.

The Process

From where you are —
to where you're meant to be.

01

Apply & Connect

Tell us about your business and ambitions. We match you with the right programme, track, and community for where you are.

02

Strategic Assessment

Our team conducts a deep-dive into your business to uncover gaps, strengths, and the highest-leverage growth opportunities.

03

Execute with Support
